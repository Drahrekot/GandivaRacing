Who We Are

Engineering EV Performance from the Ground Up

Gandiva Racing is the electric karting team of Sri Krishna College of Engineering & Technology (SKCET), Coimbatore. We design, build, and race electric go-karts in national-level competitions.

Our interdisciplinary team spans electrical systems, chassis fabrication, powertrain tuning, embedded electronics, and aerodynamics — all student-led, all hands-on.

About Gandiva Racing

More Than a Team.
A Philosophy.

Gandiva Racing was founded on a simple belief: that real engineering education happens when you build something that has to survive a racetrack. We're not a club — we're a racing outfit operating out of Sri Krishna College of Engineering & Technology, Coimbatore.

Everything on our kart is designed and fabricated by students. From the BMS to the chassis weld, from motor controller tuning to braking balance — every decision is ours to make and ours to defend on the track.

We compete under national regulations that demand technical documentation, design scrutineering, and on-track performance — all in the same weekend.
