About Gandiva Racing

More than
a team. A
philosophy.

Gandiva Racing was founded on a simple belief: that real engineering education happens when you build something that has to survive a racetrack. We're not a club — we're a racing outfit operating out of SKCET, Coimbatore.

Everything on our kart is designed and fabricated by students. From the BMS to the chassis weld, from motor controller tuning to braking balance — every decision is ours to make and ours to defend on track.

We compete under national regulations that demand technical documentation, design scrutineering, and on-track performance — all in the same weekend.
