About Gandiva Racing

More than
a team.
A philosophy.

Gandiva Racing was founded on a simple belief: that real engineering education happens when you build something that has to survive a racetrack.

Everything on our kart is designed and fabricated by students. From the BMS to the chassis weld, every decision is ours to make and defend on track.

We compete under national regulations demanding technical documentation, design scrutineering, and on-track performance — all in the same weekend.
